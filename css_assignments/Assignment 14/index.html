Cargo & Ship Survey

In survey and inspection department, our surveyors and supervisors assist in loading & discharging of cargo, and other equipment such as vehicles, containers, steel pipes / coils / plates / beams / angles / structures etc. we have tally supervisors who arrange counting of cargo at port yard and identify marks & numbers in order to segregate cargo for specific receivers. For complex loading & discharging operation, we appoint port captain who supervises heavy lift cargoes by formulating stowage plan, arrange sufficient trucks & labours and thoroughly inspect required space at port yard for uninterrupted cargo movement.

For vessel inspection, we conduct draft survey, bunker & condition survey, pre-shipment survey, h&m survey, pre-purchase survey, and marine claims survey.
